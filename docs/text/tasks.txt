Day(s)

Week(s)
Recur by weeks.
Example: If Recur Interval is 3, the task recurs every 3 weeks.
If you want a task to recur during a specific week of each month, use the ‚ÄúNth Weekday of Month‚Äù option instead.
Month(s)
Recur by months.
Example: If Recur Interval is 2, the task recurs every other month.
Tip: Use a Recur Interval of 3 to create a ‚ÄúQuarterly‚Äù recur schedule.
Month(s) on the First Weekday
Recur by months, always on the first weekday of the month (Mon, Tues, Weds, Thurs, or Fri).
Month(s) on the Last Weekday
Recur by months, always on the last weekday of the month (Mon, Tues, Weds, Thurs, or Fri).
Month(s) on the Last Day
Recur by months, always on the last day of the month.
Example: If Recur Interval is 2, and Due is December 31, Next Due will be February 28 (or 29 on a leap year).
Year(s)
