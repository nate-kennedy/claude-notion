Adding new properties to the
databases
in Ultimate Brain is easy, and it will allow you to extend and customize your template for your own needs.
In the video below, I’ll show you exactly how to do this. We’ll work through an example, where we’ll add a new
Start Date
property to the
Projects
database.
In addition to simply creating the property, we’ll add a Timeline view to the Projects page in order to take advantage of the new property.
Here’s how to add a new property at a glance:
First,
unlock your target database
. Then, open any page in the database, find the
Add a Property
button, and add your new property.
Once you’re done, I recommend
re-locking
your database.

property panel
